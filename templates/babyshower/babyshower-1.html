💛 Valaikaapu — Bangle Ceremony

An ancient and beautiful tradition of love

The Valaikaapu (Bangle ceremony) is one of the most joyful and colorful rituals of the Seemantham celebration. Family women lovingly adorn the expectant mother's wrists with glass bangles in vibrant colors — each color carrying its own blessing and deep symbolism.

The melodic clinking of bangles is believed to be the baby's first music, soothing and comforting the child in the womb. It also signals to the community that a new life is about to join the family — a cause for great celebration.

"The mother's bangles are the first songs her child hears — a melody of love, protection, and joy woven through the tapestry of generations."
